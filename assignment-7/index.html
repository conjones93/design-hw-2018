An excerpt from
“Friday Night Lights: A Town, a Team, and a Dream”
By Buzz Bissinger



Chapter 1: Odessa



In the beginning, on a dog-day Monday in the middle of August when the West Texas heat congealed in the sky, there were only the stirrings of dreams. It was the very first official day of practice and it marked the start of a new team, a new year, a new season, with a new rallying cry scribbled madly in the backs of yearbooks and on the rear windows of cars:
GOIN' TO STATE IN EIGHTY-EIGHT!

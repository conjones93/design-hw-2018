Instructable: How To Dance

Step-by-step instructions on how to dance like a pro!

Step 1 · Step 2 · Step 3






Step 1: Keep Your Balance & Know Your Capabilities



  • Not everyone can dance like Michael Jackson. With that being said, while dancing: be diligent and mindful of the space around you to ensure safety. Don't do moves that you cannot physical do... Cater your dance routine to nobody but you!
  • Set your center of gravity. We don't want to fall ovr while dance. Rather, we want to look smooth. Therefore we need to keep our center of gravity to stay on our feet and remain graceful.
  • Use your core! This your time to not only develop a sexy six-pack, but also to stay balanced, graceful and in-control. Also, using the core can relieve unneccesarry tension and use of other muscles in the body.

Step 2: Feel The Music



  • Listen to the music you are about to dance to
  • Let your heart and spirit guide your intuition, move-to-move
  • LET GO! Have fun and let the music flow your body. Be in harmony with the music

Step 3: Don't Let Them Hips Lie!



  • Try not to be self-conscious about your movements. At the end of the day nobody is judging you anyway... So shake it like you mean it!
  • Moving the hips in a smooth and dynamic way creates more color to the dance. This goes back to "use your core" - use your core to shake your hips.
  • Stay in control with hip movements. You don't want to get hurt. Just do what you can. And remember, hips don't lie!
